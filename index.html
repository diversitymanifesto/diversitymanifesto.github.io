Diversity and Inclusion Manifesto

"It's like the Agile Manifesto, but for humans."

Goal

The goal of the Manifesto is to create a set of principles, based on best practices and learnings from the Games Industry. When published, the Manifesto will be accessible to everyone. Through sharing our successes and failures we can help create a more inclusive, diverse and welcoming environment for everyone.

Scope

Over the next 12 months we will be reaching out to a number of studios and games publishers. We are hoping to gather details of their D&I efforts, centralize these details and make them available to everyone. We will ensure due diligence is taken and there will no publishing of information without the full consent of everyone involved. We'll also be gathering feedback from leaders in this field.

When we have collected sufficient information we will look at areas of overlap, what worked well, what needs tweaked and so on. At this point everyone involved in the initiative will meet to work on creating an early draft of the Manifesto.

We need help

This is a community effort in which everyone benefits. There will of course be some push back and criticism. For anyone who thinks this is a terrible idea then you are especially welcome to help us find alternative avenues. The "Diversity and Inclusion Manifesto" make take years to define and it will constantly be evolving as does our industry and society.

If you would like to get involved then drop a mail to : info@diversitymanifesto.org
Please help us spread the word about this initiative. Everyone is welcome!
Thank you.
