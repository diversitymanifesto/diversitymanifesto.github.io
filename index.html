Diversity and Inclusion Manifesto

"It's like the Agile Manifesto, but for humans. " @dandimanifesto

What Is The Diversity & Inclusion Manifesto ?

It is a set of values and principles created collaboratively within the Games Industry.

It is a rallying call to the Games Industry to advance diversity and inclusion within the workplace by working collectively.

It is the foundation on which we can create a dynamic, diverse and inclusive workplace where employees feel fearless, valued, and excited about building their career within the Games Industry.

What The Diversity & Inclusion Manifesto Is Not ?

The Diversity and Inclusion Manifesto is not a cookie cutter, one size fits all solution. It is a baseline, a foundation on which studios and publishers can create their own goals.

How Can We Sign Up ?

We are initially reaching out to games studios and publishers with whom we have worked in the past.

We will ask each studio and publisher to sign up to this initiative and to commit to the following 2 statements : 1 : We will continue to make our workplaces trusting places to have complex, and sometimes difficult, conversations about diversity and inclusion 2 : We will share best—and unsuccessful—practices We will use these 2 commitments to generate conversation between signatories. (Source : www.ceoaction.com) As our list of signatories grows we will invite other studios and publishers to sign up.

If you would like to sign up drop a mail to : signup@diversitymanifesto.org and we will get in touch.

What's Next ?

We plan to meet quarterly with a representative from each of the signatories. During these meetings we will talk about progress being made, best practices, unsuccessful practices and discuss any D&I issues which may be useful for other signatories.

At the end of 2019 we will gather all the representatives together at a central location and we will prepare the first draft of the Manifesto.
